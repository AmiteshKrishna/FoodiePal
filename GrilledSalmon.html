Grilled Salmon with Asparagus

A healthy and delicious recipe for grilled salmon served with fresh asparagus, perfect for a quick and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the asparagus and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the salmon and asparagus on the grill.
  5. Grill the salmon for 5-6 minutes per side until cooked through.
  6. Grill the asparagus for 3-4 minutes, turning occasionally.
  7. Remove from the grill and drizzle lemon juice over the salmon.
  8. Serve the salmon with the asparagus on the side. Enjoy!
